About the App

Welcome to the To-Do List App! This application is designed to help you organize and manage your tasks efficiently. Whether you're managing your daily tasks or planning out bigger projects, this app aims to keep you productive and on track.

Features of the App

  1. Add new tasks with custom labels to personalize your task list.
  2. Mark tasks as complete or incomplete with a single click, helping you track progress.
  3. Delete tasks you no longer need to keep your list tidy and relevant.
  4. Persistent task list using LocalStorage, ensuring your tasks remain saved even after refreshing the page.

How to Use the To-Do List App

  1. Enter your task in the text box located at the top of the main page and click the "Add Task" button. This will add the task to your list.
  2. To mark a task as complete, simply click the checkbox next to the task. The task will be visually marked as complete.
  3. If you wish to delete a task, click the "Delete" button next to the task you want to remove.
